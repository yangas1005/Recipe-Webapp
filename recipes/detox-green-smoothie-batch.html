Detox Green Smoothie Batch

Source: Instagram reel
Creator: stephaniekimchi · Type: Smoothie / Batch Prep / Breakfast

Per Serving × 14

  • 1 cup spinach
  • 1 kiwi, peeled and sliced
  • 1/2 cup cucumber, sliced
  • 1/4 avocado, peeled
  • Lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds
  • Coconut water (to blend when serving)

Method

Prep and freeze 14 individual smoothie portions. When ready to drink, add coconut water and blend.

The reel describes this as a two-week detox smoothie batch to make mornings easier with one prep day.
