Immunity-Boosting Wellness Shots

Source: Instagram reel
Creator: benjixavierr · Type: Wellness Drink / Batch Prep / Shots

Ingredients

  • 2 peeled lemons
  • 2 peeled oranges
  • A knob of ginger
  • 2 cups coconut water
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • Tiny pinch of black pepper

Method

Blend everything together, strain if you want it smoother, and store in the fridge for the week.

The reel says it makes about 10–12 shots depending on pour size.
