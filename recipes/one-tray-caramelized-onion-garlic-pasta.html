One-Tray Caramelized Onion & Garlic Pasta

Source: Instagram reel
Creator: recipeincaption · Type: Pasta / One-Tray / Comfort Food

Ingredients

  • 2 large onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 whole garlic head
  • 2 tbsp chili crisp
  • 1 1/2 cups cream or coconut milk
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce (optional)
  • 1/2 cup fresh Parmesan, grated
  • 1/2 lemon, juiced
  • 12 oz (340 g) pasta of choice
  • 5 tbsp olive oil
  • Pasta water, as needed
  • 1 tbsp paprika
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 2 tsp pepper
  • 2 tsp Italian seasoning

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C). Add sliced onions to a baking dish and toss with paprika, garlic powder, salt, pepper, Italian seasoning, and 4 tbsp olive oil.
  2. Slice the top off the garlic head, drizzle with 1 tbsp olive oil, sprinkle with salt, and place cut-side up on the onions.
  3. Cover with foil and bake for 1 hour, tossing onions every 20 minutes and adding a splash of water each time to prevent burning.
  4. Cook pasta according to package directions. Reserve up to 1 cup pasta water before draining.
  5. Remove garlic, cool slightly, and squeeze roasted garlic into the onions.
  6. Add chili crisp, cream or coconut milk, soy sauce if using, lemon juice, Parmesan, and cooked pasta. Toss together, adding pasta water as needed until glossy and saucy.
  7. Finish with extra Parmesan, chili crisp, and parsley if desired.
