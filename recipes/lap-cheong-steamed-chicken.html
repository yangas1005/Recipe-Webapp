Lap Cheong Steamed Chicken

Source: Instagram reel
Creator: Sharilyn (@cook.n.plate)
Type: Chicken / Steamed / Chinese

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless chicken thighs
  • 2 Chinese sausages
  • 1 handful black fungus
  • 4–5 dried shiitake mushrooms
  • 2–3 tbsp ginger juice

Marinade

  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tsp pepper
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil

Method

  • Combine chicken with the marinade.
  • Add Chinese sausage, black fungus, shiitake mushrooms, and ginger juice.
  • Steam over high heat for 15–20 minutes until chicken is fully cooked.

Garnish

  • Fresh coriander
  • Optional chili oil
