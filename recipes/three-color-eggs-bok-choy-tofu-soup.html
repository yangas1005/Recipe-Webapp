Three-Color Eggs with Bok Choy & Tofu Soup

Source: Instagram reel
Creator: FeiBaBa (@feibabacooks)
Type: Soup / Cantonese / Tofu

Ingredients

  • 4 oz bok choy
  • 1 box soft tofu (12 oz)
  • 2 century eggs
  • 1 salted egg
  • 1 egg
  • 6 pieces dried scallops
  • 3–4 slices ginger
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 3–4 cups water
  • 1 tsp chicken bouillon powder
  • 1/4 tsp white pepper powder

Notes

The caption included the ingredient list but not a full method breakdown. This looks like a comforting Cantonese-style soup built around tofu, bok choy, century egg, salted egg, and dried scallops.
