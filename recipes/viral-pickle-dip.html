Viral Pickle Dip

Source: Instagram reel
Creator: low.carb.love · Type: Dip / Appetizer / High Protein

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Greek yogurt
  • 4 ounces whipped cream cheese
  • 2 scoops Foundational Daily Fiber
  • 1-2 cups shredded chicken
  • 1/2 cup bacon or turkey bacon
  • 4 pickle spears, diced
  • 1/3 cup green onion
  • 1/4 cup diced jalapeño
  • 1 tablespoon ranch seasoning

Notes

A high-protein, high-fiber pickle dip reel shared as a game day / Super Bowl appetizer idea.
