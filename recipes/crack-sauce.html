Crack Sauce

Source: Instagram reel
Creator: jess.taye · Type: Sauce / Thai-inspired / Condiment

Ingredients

  • 1/2 bunch mint
  • 1/2 bunch cilantro
  • 2 limes
  • 2 stalks lemongrass
  • 6-10 red bird chilies
  • 4 tbsp fish sauce
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 6 tbsp light brown sugar
  • 1/4 large shallot
  • 10 cloves garlic

Method

Put all ingredients into a blender and blend to your desired consistency.

If you follow the base recipe, it yields about 12 ounces. The reel notes the video version is doubled and makes about 24 ounces.
