Green Juice

Source: Instagram reel
Creator: Tina Nunez (@thetinanunez)
Type: Drink / Juice / Wellness

Ingredients

  • 4 kale leaves
  • 2 handfuls spinach
  • 2 cucumbers
  • 6 stalks celery
  • 3 apples
  • 2 lemons
  • 2 inches fresh ginger root

Yield

Makes about 40 ounces of juice.

Notes

Caption describes this as a fresh green juice meant to help with detox, skin clarity, and a healthy glow.
