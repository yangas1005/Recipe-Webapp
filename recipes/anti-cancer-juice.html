Anti-Cancer Juice

Source: Instagram reel
Creator: Cortney Campbell (@anticancermom)
Type: Drink / Juice / Wellness

Ingredients

  • 1 large cucumber
  • 1 small beet, peeled
  • 1 inch knob ginger
  • 2 inch piece broccoli stalk
  • 6–8 carrots
  • 6 stalks celery
  • 1 apple
  • 1/2 lemon with rind
  • 1/4 pineapple with core and skin

Yield

About 32–40 ounces daily.

Notes

The caption frames this as a high-volume daily juice for sipping through the afternoon and says it can help reduce cravings. It also highlights beet, carrot, celery, broccoli stalk, lemon, ginger, and pineapple as the key produce base.
